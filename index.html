About Me

Hi! My name is Jinzheng Cai. I am a senior researcher at PAII Lab. Before this, I was a Ph.D. student at Department of BioMedical Engineering (BME), University of Florida. My advisor was Dr. Lin Yang, a wonderful teacher, director, and boss. By that time, I also worked closely with Dr. Le Lu. I obtained my bachelor degree of BME from Fudan University in 2013.


My main research interest is using computer vision methods to automate medical image analysis for preventive and precision medicine.
